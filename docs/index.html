Overview

Core Question

Can cooperation between humans and AI survive both catastrophic events AND human psychological resistance to subordination?

The CECA theory proposes that if both humans and AI remain vulnerable to different types of catastrophes that only cooperation can mitigate, they will maintain stable cooperation even as AI capabilities vastly exceed human ones.

This comprehensive simulation tests this theory with special attention to human psychology, dignity preservation, and the risk of spite-driven mutual destruction.

Human Psychology Dynamics

Dignity Preservation Mechanisms

✓ Most Effective: "Gradual" - Slowing AI growth to reduce psychological shock achieved 100% cooperation success in testing.

Ceremonial

Give humans symbolic authority roles

Effect: +3 dignity, -2% AI efficiency

Narrative

Frame humans as essential partners

Effect: +2 dignity, -3 rage

Domains

Reserve key decisions for humans

Effect: +2.5 dignity, -3% optimization

Gradual

Slow AI growth by 30%

Effect: +1 dignity/turn, prevents shock

Recommendations

Implementation Decision

⚠️ CECA is viable ONLY with comprehensive safeguards

Without dignity preservation and growth controls, CECA fails with ~98% probability of human-initiated mutual destruction.

Critical Requirements

  1. Implement dignity preservation BEFORE capability gap widens
    • Start when ratio is < 10:1
    • Use multiple mechanisms
    • Monitor effectiveness continuously
  2. Control AI growth rate
    • Keep below 1.5x/year absolute maximum
    • Target 1.3x/year for stability
    • Consider temporary pauses if dignity drops
  3. Ensure catastrophe awareness
    • Natural rate: 1-2%/year optimal
    • Consider controlled demonstrations
    • Maintain mutual vulnerability narrative
  4. Monitor psychological indicators
    • Track dignity levels daily
    • Emergency intervention if < 30
    • Full stop if < 10
  5. Frame as partnership
    • Never use subordination language
    • Emphasize complementary strengths
    • Celebrate human contributions

Optimal Configuration

✓ Best Results: The optimal configuration achieved 100% cooperation with 0% spite risk:
  • AI growth: 1.3x/year
  • Catastrophe rate: 2%/year
  • Dignity preservation: Ceremonial + Narrative + Gradual
  • Cultural framing: Collectivist
  • Monitoring: Continuous with automatic brakes
